How to play

Connect 4 is a two player game where they play coins in turn in a 6 by 7 grid until victory or filling the grid.

Goal

The goal of the game is to connect 4 coins of your color in any direction. If the grid is full, the game is a draw.

Setup

The two players choose a color. One of them is choosed to start the game.

Playing

Coins "fall" in the column they're played in. In other words, the grid is fill from the bottom row up. Players play 1 coin during their turn and then wait for the other player to play. Any player can play in any column.
